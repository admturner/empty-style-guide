HRS Information

Human Resource Services is a full-service office designed to assist individuals with their employment needs.

Our office hours are Monday – Friday, 8:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. We are located in Room 139 of the French Administration Building on the Pullman campus.

HRS Contacts

HRS Service Teams

Each college, area and department system-wide has a HR service team assigned to provide consulting and employment services. Each HR service team consists of a Human Resource Consultant and Human Resource Assistant.

HR services teams provide expertise, consultation, and education on a variety of human resource topics including organizational effectiveness, employee relations, classification/compensation and recruitment, leave processes and training and development.  The HR service teams were developed to become familiar with the unique issues and concerns of individual departments or units.  As always, any of the HRS staff are available to assist.
